Zoomdeck is an innovative platform for great conversations around photos. By letting you add interactive tags to any photo, Zoomdeck enables meaningful conversations right there on the photo.

Deepak Prakash has been hacking since he was 12. A few years back, he picked up a passion for photography and a combination of the two led to the idea for Zoomdeck. He was one of the first members of Apache Design India R&D team and spent 4.5 years developing algorithms and data structures to model complex chip designs. He has been placed in the top 200 at Google Code Jam and is also a recipient of Seagate photography award.
