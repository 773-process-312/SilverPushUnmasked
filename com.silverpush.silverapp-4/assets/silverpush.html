SilverPush is the first company to allow app owners and ad networks to intelligently connect with mobile users. Until now, app owners have struggled to monetize their apps due to infrequent usage, while brands have been reluctant to advertise on mobile due to limited user data. SilverPush algorithms analyze multiple sources of data, and identify the most lucrative opportunities to deliver targeted content to users via push notifications, SMS, email and more.
